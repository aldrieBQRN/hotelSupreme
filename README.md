**Hotel Supreme** is a comprehensive web-based Hotel Management System (HMS) designed to streamline daily hotel operations. It handles room management, guest reservations, inventory tracking, and housekeeping inspections with a unified, user-friendly interface.

---

## Key Features

### Dashboard
* **Real-time Overview:** View total guests, occupied rooms, pending damage reports, and low stock alerts at a glance.
* **Quick Actions:** Fast access to check-ins, inspections, and reports.

### Room Management
* **Status Tracking:** Monitor rooms statuses: *Vacant, Occupied, Reserved,* or *Maintenance*.
* **CRUD Operations:** Add, edit, and delete room details easily.
* **Filtering:** Filter rooms by type, status, or search by room number.

### Guest Management
* **Check-in/Check-out:** Seamless process for checking guests in and out.
* **Booking Details:** Track booking sources (Agoda, Booking.com, Walk-in), meal plans, and guest pax.
* **History:** Maintain a record of past guest stays.

* **Stock Tracking:** Monitor item quantities and categories (Linens, Toiletries, etc.).
* **Low Stock Alerts:** Visual indicators for items below reorder points.
* **Restocking:** Simple interface to add stock or update item details.

### Housekeeping & Inspections
* **Digital Checklist:** Interactive form for inspecting room items.
* **Damage Reporting:** Log damaged, missing, or replacement-needed items directly during inspection.
* **Automated Status:** Room status automatically updates to "Maintenance" if critical issues are found.

### Reports & Analytics
* **Visual Charts:** Interactive Doughnut and Line charts for issue types and resolution trends.
* **Data Export:** Export reports to **Excel** or **PDF** (via Print).
* **Issue Resolution:** Track and resolve reported damages, automatically deducting replacements from inventory.

## Tech Stack

* **Backend:** PHP (Native)
* **Database:** MySQL
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla & jQuery)
* **Libraries:**
    * [SweetAlert2](https://sweetalert2.github.io/) (Beautiful popup notifications)
    * [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/) (Data visualization)
    * [Select2](https://select2.org/) (Enhanced dropdowns)
    * [Flatpickr](https://flatpickr.js.org/) (Date pickers)
    * [FontAwesome](https://fontawesome.com/) (Icons)
